A cross-verified database of notable people, from 3500BC to 2018AD

The task of registering famous individuals and their influence has been a recurrent field of study for over two thousand years. Over the past few years, this task has expanded to a much larger scale, due to a growing number of databases documenting history, allowing statistical analysis of socio-historical facts at an unprecedented scale. Our most recent paper develops a cross-verified database of 2.2 million notable individuals using several Wikipedia editions and Wikidata.
